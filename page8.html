About me
Strauss

My name is Johanna Méndez and I am passionate about helping people and companies through design. I try constantly to improve and learn something new every day. I am detailed and organized and also, I enjoy the adventure of working with new teams and embarking on new projects.
I have a degree in web design and multimedia but since most days I am also self-taught, I have learned about other areas of design. My professional experience has been extensive in dealing with customer service for various companies, but personally and design wise I have been able to participate in various projects such as the creation of branding books for companies, illustrations and costume design.
