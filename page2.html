Mushroom Girl

Mushroom girl is a free-hand drawing developed in class. Its purpose was to create an illustration that can be used in products targeted for girls under the age of 10. 




A vectorized version of the image was created using Adobe Illustrator and then colorize in Photoshop.



In addition, two different backgrounds were created to be used in the different version of some of the products.
